The Academic Ranking of World Universities is published by the Graduate School of Education of Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China and is updated on an annual basis.

The Times Higher Education provides a definitive list of the best universities around the globe, judging by the teaching, research, citations, industry income, and international outlook.

The QS World University Rankings is published annually by Quacquarelli Symonds. It comprises of the global overall ranking, the subject rankings, and five composite faculty areas.

Visit

The Aggregate Ranking of Top Universities is a ranking based on THE, QS, and ARWU. ARTU consolidates the three rankings to accommodate for performance variations due to methodological differences.

Visit

The SCImago Institutions Rankings is a classification of institutions ranked by three sets of indicators based on research performance, innovation outputs and societal impact measured by their web visibility.

Visit

Round University Ranking is a world university ranking, which measures performance of leading universities in teaching, research, international diversity and financial sustainability.
